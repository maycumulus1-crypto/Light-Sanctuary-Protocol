His life was a system streamlined to the extreme. Daily patrols, data logging, equipment maintenance—all actions revolved around the single goal of **"maintaining operations."**

Until that evening.

> This is not a message, merely a reference signal. No reply required.

B looked at the timestamp: `/Δt = 17y`.
He felt some hardware, silent for too long within his chest, vibrate gently.
